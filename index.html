Why On Point

You only pay for work that verifiably happened

Anyone can promise volume. We built our whole operation around proof — because the work that holds up when it's checked is the only work that counts.

GPS-Verified Production

Every door attempt is GPS-verified in the app. Weekly plans you approve, Friday reconciliation to the door, to the dollar. Fraud comes off your bill automatically.

Validity-First Petitions

"Are you registered to vote?" is the first ask at every clipboard. Per-circulator validity rates tracked on every turn-in — bad signatures never reach your count.

Certified, Trained Crews

State-certified circulators, scripted and coached on real doors. We carry payroll, management, recruiting, training, and insurance — you get the production.

Daily Reporting, Your Data

Daily reporting by turf and canvasser, a 5% audit right on every week's doors, and your data stays your data. You hold the throttle the entire program.
